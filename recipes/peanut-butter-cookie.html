Peanut Butter Cookies Recipe

Description

The classic peanut butter cookies recipe on AllRecipes features simple ingredients like peanut butter, sugar, and flour. The cookies are known for their chewy texture and rich peanut butter flavor. A common method includes rolling the dough into balls, placing them on a baking sheet, and pressing a fork on top to create a signature crisscross pattern. For more details and specific baking instructions, you can check out the full recipe here.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking soda
  • Baking powder
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Egg
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix the flour, baking soda, and baking powder in a small bowl.
  3. Cream butter and sugar together until smooth. Add the egg and vanilla extract.
  4. Gradually blend the dry ingredients into the wet mixture.
  5. Roll the dough into small balls and place them on a baking sheet.
  6. Bake for 8-10 minutes until the edges turn golden.
