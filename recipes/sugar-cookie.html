Sugar Cookie recipes

Description

An easy sugar cookie recipe usually starts with creaming together butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Eggs and vanilla extract are then beaten in to add richness and flavor. In a separate bowl, dry ingredients like flour, baking powder, and salt are whisked together. The dry ingredients are gradually mixed into the wet ingredients to form a soft dough. The dough is often chilled for a short time to make it easier to handle. Then it's rolled into small balls or dropped by spoonfuls onto baking sheets. The cookies are baked in a preheated oven until the edges are lightly golden, usually about 8-10 minutes. After cooling on the baking sheet for a few minutes, they're transferred to a wire rack to cool completely. The result is a batch of tender, sweet cookies with a slightly crisp exterior and soft interior, perfect for decorating or enjoying as-is.
